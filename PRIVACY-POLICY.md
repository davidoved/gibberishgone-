# GibberishGone Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2, 2026**

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any user data.** All text conversion happens entirely within your browser. No text, personal information, or usage data is ever sent to any server.

## Permissions Explained

- **clipboardWrite**: Only used to copy converted text to your clipboard when you click the Copy button.
- **activeTab**: Only used when you right-click selected text on a page to convert it.
- **storage**: Only used to remember your preferred default keyboard layouts locally.
- **contextMenus**: Only used to add the "Fix wrong keyboard layout" right-click option.
- **scripting**: Only used to replace selected text on the current page with the converted version.

## Third Parties

We do not collect, store, or share the text you type into our tool.

However, this website displays advertisements served by **Google AdSense**, a third-party advertising service. Google may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. This may include your IP address, browser type, and browsing behavior.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites that use its services at [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites). You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).
